Superstition

2025 Digital Print Giclée Paper Limited Edition

About the Work

"Superstition" is a striking piece that explores the deep bond between humans and dogs through the lens of photographic artistry. This work showcases the artist's signature use of rich blacks and subtle contrasts to highlight the emotional depth and spiritual connection that exists between species.

Created using photography with digital manipulation, this piece demonstrates the artist's technical skill in capturing the essence of canine companionship while maintaining the artistic integrity of the original vision. The work is printed on high-quality giclée paper to ensure the finest reproduction of the original digital artwork.

Technique

This piece employs a combination of traditional photography and contemporary digital techniques. The artist begins with carefully composed photographs, then applies digital manipulation to enhance the emotional impact while preserving the authentic character of the subject. The final result is printed using giclée technology for archival quality and exceptional color fidelity.

Inspiration

Inspired by the artist's passion for dog welfare and the historical significance of dogs in art, "Superstition" represents a modern interpretation of the timeless relationship between humans and their canine companions. The title references the deep, almost mystical connection that many people feel with their dogs—a bond that transcends the ordinary and touches something profound in the human spirit.
